Traces of the Spanish Civil War

Geolocated catalog of Spanish war vestiges

Traces of the Spanish Civil War is an app for history enthusiasts seeking to explore sites where events from the civil war took place and still persist today.

The app also features a catalog of geolocated photos from that era, along with a description of the street map connected to the conflict.

  • Over 3000 entries in the map

  • Vestiges all over the planet. Not just in contemporary Spain boundaries

  • Users can send entries

  • Around 20000 downloads
